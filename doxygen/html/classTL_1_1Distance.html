Computes the asymmetric Hausdorff distance between two triangle meshes.

This function computes the asymmetric Hausdorff distance between two triangle meshes, mesh and target_mesh. The distance is calculated by finding the maximum distance from any point on mesh to its closest point on target_mesh.

Parameters
meshThe first triangle mesh from which points are sampled.
target_meshThe second triangle mesh to which distances are calculated.
precisionDesired precision for the computed asymmetric Hausdorff distance, influencing the sampling density on the mesh.
Returns
double The computed asymmetric Hausdorff distance.

Computes the Hausdorff distance between two triangle meshes.

This function computes the Hausdorff distance between two triangle meshes, mesh1 and mesh2. The distance is calculated as the maximum of the asymmetric Hausdorff distances computed in both directions: from mesh1 to mesh2 and from mesh2 to mesh1.
